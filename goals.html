Swiss Brakeman on the Rise

All In for the Olympics

My Goals

My goal is to qualify for the Olympic Games next winter as a brakeman for Switzerland. I’m focused on building the speed and power it takes to compete with the best — and setting a start record would be a dream come true. After the Games, I plan to start driving and build my own team. I want to grow with that crew, compete at the highest level, and see how far we can go together. This is just the beginning — and I'm all in.
